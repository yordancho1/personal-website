AUXIT Music Platform

2025
Music Tech / AI
AI Application
Personal Project

AUXIT was an ambitious personal project exploring the intersection of artificial intelligence and music discovery. The platform concept aimed to revolutionize how people experience music by leveraging advanced AI to create hyper-personalized listening experiences. This comprehensive project involved developing sophisticated machine learning algorithms, cross-platform API integration, and a complete user experience design for modern music discovery.

Core Features

Hyper-Personalized Playlists

Advanced AI analyzes saved songs, playlists, and listening habits to create perfectly tailored music recommendations that evolve with user preferences.

Mood-Based Discovery

Real-time mood detection algorithms generate perfect playlists matching users' emotional state, creating the ideal soundtrack for any moment.

Dynamic Playlists

Experience playlists that intelligently adapt based on time of day, weather conditions, and current activity for contextually relevant music.

Discovery Mode

Explore new artists and genres with AI-powered recommendations that balance familiarity with discovery, expanding musical horizons safely.

Cross-Platform Integration

Seamless connection with major streaming services including Spotify and Apple Music, unifying music libraries across platforms.

Concert Recommendations

Personalized concert and event suggestions based on music preferences and location, connecting digital listening with live experiences.
