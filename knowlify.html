Knowlify (KNOW Token)

2024 - 2025
Blockchain / EdTech
Web3 Application
Personal Project

Knowlify was a passion project exploring the intersection of AI-powered education and blockchain technology. The platform concept featured the KNOW token, a Solana-based SPL utility token designed to reward users for completing educational content, participating in community events, and contributing knowledge. This comprehensive project involved designing token economics, smart contract architecture, gamification systems, and a full educational platform ecosystem.

Key Features

Token Earning System

Users earn KNOW tokens by completing quizzes, participating in community events, creating content, and referring friends. The gamified approach makes learning engaging and rewarding.

AI Learning Assistant

Advanced AI-powered tools help personalize learning paths, provide instant feedback, and create customized study materials based on individual learning styles.

Staking & Governance

Token holders can stake KNOW to earn interest, unlock exclusive features, and participate in platform governance decisions through decentralized voting.

Premium Content Access

Spend tokens to unlock in-depth articles, video tutorials, advanced study tools, and exclusive educational resources from industry experts.

Real-World Rewards

Redeem KNOW tokens for gift cards, educational material discounts, and other tangible rewards, bridging the gap between digital learning and real value.

Community Driven

Join discussion forums, study groups, and participate in expert Q&A sessions. Build your reputation and earn tokens through knowledge sharing.
